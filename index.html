About Me

I am Harsh Rajput – an author, social entrepreneur, and mentor. My journey began early in life as a class representative and evolved through my role as a campus ambassador at Gujarat Technological University. I founded Tarang, a social startup developing medtech devices for people with speech and cognitive disabilities, earning accolades such as the “Gandhian Engg Principles National Design Award”, “TiE Awards” and “Best Student Startup.”

I also serve as a Mentor at Reliance Foundation, guiding over 20,000 Reliance Undergraduate Scholars; I lead the International Communication team of ISWIT and work as an Ambassador for SOrCE – organizing international student festivals and conferences. Additionally, I am honored with the Student Award for Leadership & Excellence by Gujarat Technological University and have represented Gujarat at VBYLD & SOUL while winning three national hackathons.
